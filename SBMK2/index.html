Chocolate Chip Muffins Recipe
  • Ingredients for 12 Muffins
  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • ⅓ cup vegetable oil (or melted butter)
  • 1 large egg
  • ⅓–½ cup milk (adjust for batter consistency)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

1. Preheat oven: to 200°C (400°F). Line a 12-cup muffin tin with paper liners.

2. Mix dry ingredients: In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, salt, and baking powder.

3. Prepare wet mixture: In a separate bowl, whisk oil, egg, milk, and vanilla until smooth.

4. Combine: Pour wet mixture into dry ingredients. Stir gently until just combined (avoid overmixing).

5. Add chocolate chips: Fold in chocolate chips evenly.

6. Fill muffin cups: Divide batter among liners, filling about ¾ full. Sprinkle extra chips on top if desired.

7. Bake: Place in oven for 18–22 minutes, until golden and a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.

8. Cool & enjoy: Let muffins rest in the pan for 5 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack.

Tip: For richer flavor, replace half the flour with cocoa powder to make double chocolate muffins.

  • Bon Appetit!
