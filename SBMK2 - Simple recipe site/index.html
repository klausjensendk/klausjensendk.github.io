Risengrød med fødder
  • Ingredients (2–4 servings):
  • 2 dl water (about 200 ml)
  • 180 g short-grain rice, (grødris)
  • 1 liter whole milk
  • ½ tsp salt
  • Butter, cinnamon, and sugar for topping

Risengrød (Danish Rice Porridge) Risengrød is a traditional Danish comfort food made with short-grain rice and milk. It’s creamy, simple, and often served with butter, cinnamon, and sugar. Perfect for cold evenings or as part of Christmas traditions in Denmark.

1. Bring the water to a boil in a heavy-bottomed pot.

2. Add the rice and simmer for 2 minutes until the water is absorbed.

3. Pour in the milk gradually while stirring. Bring to a gentle boil.

4. Reduce heat immediately and let simmer on low for 35–45 minutes.

5. Stir frequently to prevent sticking. The porridge should be thick and creamy.

6. Add salt to taste once the rice is tender.

7. Serve warm with a knob of butter in the center, sprinkled with cinnamon sugar.
