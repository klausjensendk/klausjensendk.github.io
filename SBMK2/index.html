Gnocchi with Roasted Sweet Potato, Carrot & Pesto Rosso
  • 07/09/2025
  • The master: Marta
  • Summer/Winter

This vibrant gnocchi dish draws inspiration from Mediterranean and North African culinary traditions, blending Italian comfort with bold, aromatic spices. Gnocchi, a classic Italian pasta made from potatoes, provides a satisfying base rich in complex carbohydrates. The sweet potato and carrot add natural sweetness, fiber, and a boost of beta-carotene‚Äîgreat for eye health and immunity. Pesto Rosso or romesco sauce brings depth with roasted peppers, nuts, and olive oil, echoing Spanish and Catalan flavors. Ras al hanout, a Moroccan spice blend, adds warmth and complexity while supporting digestion. Fresh herbs like parsley and rosemary offer antioxidants and anti-inflammatory benefits. The tomato or cream sauce adds richness, while the cheese and bacon topping delivers indulgence in moderation. This dish is a celebration of seasonal vegetables, balanced nutrition, and global flavor. It‚Äôs perfect for cozy dinners that feel both nourishing and gourmet.

  • Serves four

  • 2 sweet potatoes (boniato), peeled and diced

  • 100 g pesto rosso or romesco sauce
  • Tomato sauce or cream (to taste)
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tsp ras al hanout
  • 1 tsp dried rosemary
  • 1 tsp mixed herbs (finas hierbas
  • Grated cheese and bacon (for gratin topping)
